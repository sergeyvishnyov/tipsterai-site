Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the TipsterAI app (“Application”) for mobile devices, created by the developer (“Service Provider”). TipsterAI helps football fans follow fixtures, view schedules and tournaments, analyze data, and chat with an AI assistant. The Application is provided “AS IS”.

Information Collection and Use

The Application may collect certain information when you download and use it, such as:

  • Device information (e.g., operating system and model)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • App interactions (e.g., screens visited, time spent)
  • App performance and crash data

The Application does not request precise (GPS-level) location.

Third-Party Services

The Application may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you in accordance with their own policies. Examples include analytics and crash reporting providers.
